"Brew & Bloom" Coffee Shop Web Design Style Guide

Introduction

This style guide outlines the visual and interactive elements of the "Brew & Bloom" coffee shop website, ensuring consistency and adherence to our brand identity. It serves as a reference for designers, developers, and content creators to maintain a unified and professional look and feel across all website pages and elements.

Brand Identity

  • Brand Name: Brew & Bloom
  • Tagline: Your Daily Dose of Caffeine & Cheer
  • Brand Personality: Warm, inviting, friendly, community-oriented, and slightly quirky.
